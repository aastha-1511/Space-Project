Major Space Missions Timeline

Sputnik 1

October 4, 1957

First artificial Earth satellite launched by the Soviet Union.

Vostok 1

April 12, 1961

First manned mission to orbit the Earth by Yuri Gagarin.

Gemini 5

August 21, 1965

First long-duration spaceflight, lasting 8 days.

Apollo 11

July 20, 1969

First manned mission to land on the Moon by NASA.

Voyager 1

September 5, 1977

Space probe launched by NASA to study the outer Solar System and interstellar space.

Hubble Space Telescope

April 24, 1990

Space telescope launched into low Earth orbit by NASA.

Moon Impact Probe

October 22, 2008

India's first lunar probe, part of Chandrayaan-1 mission.

Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM)

November 5, 2013

India's first interplanetary mission to Mars.

Mars Rover Curiosity

August 6, 2012

Rover designed to explore the Gale crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission.

SpaceX Falcon Heavy

February 6, 2018

Maiden flight of the Falcon Heavy rocket by SpaceX, the most powerful operational rocket in the world.

Chandrayaan-3

July 22, 2019

India's third lunar exploration mission.
